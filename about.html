Our Founding Story

Founded in 2001 at Moradabad by Mr. Manish Vishwakarma, Vintech Computer Education began with a singular mission: to make high-quality computer education accessible to everyone.

In 2010, we shifted to Bareilly. Today, with over two decades of experience, we are proud to have transformed over 5,000 students into skilled technical professionals working across India.

UP Government Skill Partner

Vintech Computer Education is proud to be recognized as an official Skill Partner of the Uttar Pradesh Government. This prestigious partnership is in collaboration with UPRTOU (Uttar Pradesh Rajarshi Tandon Open University).

Through this initiative, we offer certified programs that empower our students with government-recognized qualifications, enhancing their career prospects in the competitive IT sector.
