Vintech Computer Education, Since 2001

Vintech Computer Education is a State Government registered institute.

Our Founding

Vintech Computer Education was founded by Mr. Manish Vishwakarma in 2001 at Moradabad, with the aim of making computer education accessible to everyone.


In 2010, we shifted to Bareilly, but with full dedication, we continued to work hard towards our mission.


We are proud to share that in our long and exciting journey of over two decades, under the guidance and hard work of Mr. Manish Vishwakarma and our dedicated teachers and staff, Vintech Computer Education has trained over 5000 students into technical professionals working across India and leading successful lives.


We currently have 3 branches located across Bareilly:

Main Branch: 2nd Floor, Maurya Tower, Opp. Vedanta Hospital, Doraha Road, Bareilly
Second Branch:Near Golden Green Park Gate,Bareilly
Third Branch: Umedpur Bhuta, BHUTA, Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh 243503
