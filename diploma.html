How to Download Your Diploma

Follow these steps carefully to get your diploma:

  1. Enter your Roll Number in the box on this page.
  2. Click on "Find My Diploma".
  3. It will download automatically (except on Firefox)
    You need to save on firefox manually
  4. It will ask for a password.
    Your password is your RollNumber@Firstname.

Example

Roll Number: 1184
Name: Ashish Kumar
Password: 1184@Ashish
