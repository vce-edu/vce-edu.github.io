Free Laptop Scheme

At Vintech Computer Education, we know how important the right tools are for learning. That’s why we’ve introduced our Free Laptop Scheme – giving every eligible student a laptop at no extra cost!

With this initiative, our aim is to make learning easier and more convenient for every student. A personal laptop means you don’t have to wait for lab hours or shared systems — you can learn whenever and wherever you want. It gives you the freedom to practice coding, explore design, or dive into Computer skills at your own pace. More than that, it opens the door to self-study, creative projects, and personal exploration beyond the classroom, allowing you to truly grow in ways that go beyond the syllabus.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Available for students enrolled in MDCA, O Level or Web Designing courses.
  • A minimum fee submission of ₹10,000 is required before laptop handover.
  • No age limit – learning is for everyone!
