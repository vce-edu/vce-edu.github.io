Free Laptop Scheme At VCE

What is Free Laptop Scheme?

Free Laptop Scheme, is a initiative taken by Mr. Manish Vishwakarma, director and founder of Vintech Computer Education.

This scheme is motivated from our mission to make computer education accessible to everyone. As the name suggests , in this scheme we give out free laptops to our students , who are enrolled in elligible courses for this scheme.

You can read the Elligibility Criteria for the scheme.

Why we started The Scheme?

At Vintech Computer Education, we belive one can only master computer skills through constant practice. But, when it comes to praticing computer skills problem with most students especially those with financial issues or those who come from backward areas of our country is that they always cannot afford a laptop for contineous pratice, while also paying for their course. This issue become a very big hurdle while learning especially with courses including programming or any sort of coding.
We notice this problem which was being faced by many of our students , and so to full fill Vintech Compter Education (VCE)'s mission , Mr. Manish Vishwakarma started Free Laptop Scheme.

Elligibility Criteria

Elligible Courses: MDCA, O Level, Web Desgining.

DownPayment: Rs. 10,000

Note:
We are not asking extra amount for the Laptop, this DownPayment is only required as an assurance amount, when you need laptop before finishing the course and this amount will be added as your course fee payment and you will not be asked to pay full course fees, Rs. 10,000 will get deducted from total course fees.
