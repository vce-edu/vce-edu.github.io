Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Vintech Computer Education collects, uses, protects, and handles your personal information. By using our website or services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information: Name, contact number, email address, address, age, education details, and other information shared during admission or scholarship registration.

Usage Information: Pages visited, interactions, device information, browser type, and general website analytics.

Documents / Proof: If required for admission, verification, scholarship eligibility, or refund purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

– Student registration and admission processing
– Scholarship examination registration and results
– Communication regarding courses, exams, results, and updates
– Providing student support and counselling
– Improving our services and website experience
– Legal, verification, and security purposes if required

3. Google Analytics & Cookies

Our website may use cookies and tools like Google Analytics to understand visitor behavior, traffic sources, and website performance. These tools do not collect personal identity information but may track anonymous usage data.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

4. Data Protection & Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission or storage system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only:

– With your consent
– When legally required by government authorities or law enforcement
– With trusted service providers assisting in operations (while maintaining confidentiality)

6. Scholarship Examination & Counselling

Information submitted for scholarship examinations, counselling, and course selection is used only for academic and administrative purposes.

7. Refund & Document Verification

If a student requests a refund, supporting documents may be required for verification. Submitted proof will be handled securely and used only for verification purposes.

8. Third Party Links

Our website may contain external links (YouTube, Telegram, LinkedIn, payment pages, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. Users are encouraged to read their privacy policies.

9. Children / Minor Students

For school-going students, information may be provided by the student or parent/guardian. We encourage parents to supervise online activity and ensure accurate information submission.

10. Changes to This Policy

Vintech Computer Education may update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Updated policies will be posted on this page.

11. Contact Information
