Vintech Computer Education Scholarship Examination

About VCESE

Vintech Computer Education Scholarship Examination (VCESE) is an initiative to support talented and deserving students by providing them an opportunity to pursue quality computer education at highly discounted fees. Our goal is to encourage learning, reduce financial burden, and help students build a strong career in IT & Technology.

Who Can Apply?

The VCE Scholarship Examination is open for students from Class 6th to Graduation. School and college-going students who wish to enhance their computer knowledge and skills can apply for this examination. Even complete beginners who have never used a computer before are welcome, as the exam is designed to motivate learning rather than discourage students. Students preparing for professional computer courses will also benefit greatly from this opportunity.

No prior computer knowledge is required.

Scholarship Benefits

Students who perform well in the examination will be eligible to receive scholarships of up to 100% on course fees. Based on their performance, students may receive merit-based fee waivers and special discounts on selected professional computer courses such as ADCA+, MDCA, ADCA, and other career-oriented programs. Deserving candidates will also be awarded an official Certificate of Merit, recognizing their effort and achievement.

The final scholarship-eligible course and benefits will be informed to the student after the examination. Students may also contact us directly to know detailed information about available scholarship courses and benefits.

On the result day, Vintech Computer Education also conducts a free counselling session where our experienced team explains every course in detail, including syllabus, duration, career scope, job opportunities, and complete learning roadmap. Our aim is to guide students and parents toward the best learning path for their future.

Exam Pattern

The VCE Scholarship Examination will be conducted offline at the Vintech Computer Education Center. The test will consist of objective-type multiple-choice questions to ensure fair and easy evaluation. The total duration of the exam will be approximately 30 to 45 minutes, and students can attempt the paper in either Hindi or English. The exam focuses on important areas such as Basic Computer Knowledge, Reasoning & Aptitude, General Awareness, and Digital & IT Knowledge.

How to Apply?

Students can apply by filling out the online registration form and selecting their preferred examination date. After submitting the required details, candidates simply need to visit the center on the allotted date and appear for the examination. Once the test is completed, the results will be announced within 7 days, and eligible students will be informed about their scholarship benefits.

Important Dates

The registration process for the VCE Scholarship Examination is currently open throughout the year. We conduct the scholarship examination every month, so students can register anytime and appear in the next available exam cycle. After registration, students will be informed about their nearest upcoming exam schedule. Scholarship results are declared within 7 days from the date of the student's examination.

Rules & Guidelines

Students appearing for the scholarship examination must carry their Admit Slip or Registration Proof on the exam day. Identity verification may be required if needed. Any kind of unfair practice during the examination will result in disqualification. All decisions made by Vintech Computer Education regarding scholarship eligibility and selection will be considered final.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any registration fee?
A nominal registration charge may apply to help us manage examination arrangements and resources.

Can beginners apply?
Yes, absolutely! The examination is designed to be student-friendly and suitable even for those with little to no computer knowledge.

Will every student get a scholarship?
Scholarships are awarded based on performance and merit in the examination.
