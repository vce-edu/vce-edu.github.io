🎓 VCE Scholarship Examination 2025

At Vintech Computer Education, we are offering students an exciting opportunity to earn scholarships up to 100% on tuition fees.

📌 About the Exam

  • Date: 5th October 2025
  • Venu: VCE Main Branch
  • Eligibility: Open to all students (Class 7th – 12th & College)
  • Registration Fees: Rs. 50
  • Timings: 10 am onwards
  • Duration: 20 Mins
  • Questions: 50
  • Subjects: General Aptitude, Computer Awareness, Logical Reasoning
  • Exam Type: Objective(MCQs) + CBT(Computer Based Test)
