Who Can Apply?

The VCE Scholarship Examination is open to everyone eager to learn!

  • Students from Class 6th to Graduate level.
  • Complete beginners are welcome—no prior computer knowledge needed.
  • Professionals looking to upskill in specialized IT courses.
The exam is designed to motivate learning, not to discourage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any registration fee?

A nominal registration charge may apply to help us manage examination arrangements and resources.

Can beginners apply?

Yes, absolutely! The examination is designed to be student-friendly and suitable even for those with little to no computer knowledge.

Will every student get a scholarship?

Scholarships are awarded based on performance and merit in the examination. However, we strive to encourage every participant.

When are the exams conducted?

We conduct examinations every month. Once you register, we will notify you of the nearest upcoming date.
